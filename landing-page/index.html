How It Works

Three simple steps to start predicting and earning

1

Create or Join a Prediction

Start a prediction on anything that matters to you and your community.
  • "Will Burna drop a diss track before Friday?"
  • "Who wins the Lagos Derby?"
→ Predict anything from music to politics to viral memes.
2

Play with XP or Points

Choose your stakes and join the conversation.
  • Compete in XP-only mode or real prize leagues.
  • Share predictions directly from chats or comments. (Comment-to-Predict)

Earn & Flex

Build your reputation and earn rewards for accurate predictions.
  • Rack up XP, win tips, climb leaderboards, and earn digital bragging rights.
